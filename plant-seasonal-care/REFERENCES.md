# References (starting points, not gospel)

These are the sources that anchor the checklists in this folder. Use them as a baseline and adapt to your own light, water, and sanity.

## Houseplant basics
- Missouri Botanical Garden Plant Finder: https://www.missouribotanicalgarden.org/plantfinder/plantfindersearch.aspx
- Royal Horticultural Society (houseplants): https://www.rhs.org.uk/plants/houseplants
- University of Minnesota Extension (houseplants): https://extension.umn.edu/houseplants

## Holiday cactus / epiphytes
- North Carolina State Extension (holiday cactus): https://plants.ces.ncsu.edu/plants/schlumbergera-bridgesii/

## Succulents
- University of Minnesota Extension (succulents): https://extension.umn.edu/houseplants/succulents

## Herbs (basil)
- University of Illinois Extension (basil): https://extension.illinois.edu/herbs/basil

## Water quality
- EPA drinking water basics: https://www.epa.gov/ground-water-and-drinking-water
- CDC chloramine basics: https://www.cdc.gov/healthywater/drinking/public/chloramine-disinfection.html
